# Experiment Readout: Adding more authorization instances on web will drive $200M GMV/year, +183k MAU & 872K incremental login/year

Analytics DRI: Heming.Chen

Working team: Gandhi Amarnadh Tadiparthi (Web), Ishaan Kansal (Design), Saur Vasil (PM), Zohaib Hassan (EM)

Last updated: 03/21/2022

### TL;DR

**Problem & opportunity:**<mark>**Signing in and up is deprioritized on the web**</mark><mark>. Customers are prompted to access account entry on the home page (where the focus is actually address entry), or when checking out (when the benefit is low). </mark><mark>**If we add more entry points to sign in or up on web,**</mark><mark>weâ€™d be able to: (1) Provide Cx with accounts with a more personalized experience quickly (2) Allow account creation to happen earlier in the funnel (3) Get better understanding of web usage</mark>**Results Summary**- Estimated annualized GMV:**+$200M**- Estimated MAU:**+183k at 12 month exit** (+155k at the exit of 2022)

### Methodology

#### Overview

**Test mechanism:**A/B test**Test platform:**Web**Country:**Global**Experience:**DoorDash only**Target Population:**Non-logged in Cx**Test duration:**1 weeks experiment**Control/Treatment Split:**50/50

#### Testing Group & Bucketing

- Treatment (50%): Existing page

- Control (50%): Extra sign in/up entry points on Home page, explore & store page

- **Test Launch date:** 03/03/2022
